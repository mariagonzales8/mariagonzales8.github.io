ABOUT US

We have been the leading authority on intergalactic space exploration and travel for the past 177 years. We have accomplished over 2000 successful intergalactic missions which include new discoveries of the Alpha Suchensuch Sector and the Beta Omegasomething Quadrant. Our highly experienced staff design and build all of our vessels which include everything from single-pilot shuttlecraft to large-scale galaxy cruisers. Our specializations include warp coil technology, advanced matter transference, navigational database protocol, replicator innovation, and pie-eating contests. Our mission is to bring all types of beings from many different backgrounds together so that we may learn from one another instead of killing each other. We value knowledge, peace, and diversity while continuously striving for excellent pies.
